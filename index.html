About HackWithVizag

HackwithVizag is a national hackathon is conducted by Technical Club of Department of CSE of NSRIT. This event is sponsored under AICTE SPICES scheme. Technical sponsorship is by Demy Software Solutions.It is a 24-hour marathon of innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving at a national level. This high-stakes coding competition brings together the brightest minds in technology, challenging them to push their limits and create solutions that could shape the future.

Join us on this exciting journey of innovation and creativity. Let's push the boundaries of what's possible and create solutions that make a positive impact on society.

Precision Agriculture

Future farming embraces AI, robotics, and smart sensors for precision. Drones monitor crops, while robots handle tasks like planting and harvesting, operating round-the-clock. Blockchain ensures transparent supply chains, and biotechnology enhances crop resilience. This tech-driven approach optimizes efficiency and sustainability in agriculture.

Health, Agriculture and Rural Development

By integrating technology into rural sectors, we empower communities with tools for progress. Access to smart farming solutions, digital education, and e-commerce platforms bridges the gap, fostering economic growth and sustainability. This synergy ensures that the benefits of technological advancement extend to every corner, propelling rural areas into a future of opportunity and development.

Application Security

Deploying advanced encryption measures, implementing robust multi-factor authentication, and fostering a cybersecurity culture through education are vital steps in addressing cybersecurity challenges. Continuous monitoring of network vulnerabilities and prompt incident response further fortify defenses. Embracing emerging technologies like AI for threat detection ensures a proactive approach, creating a resilient digital landscape against evolving cyber threats.

Open Source Contribution

Joining the open-source movement fosters collaboration and knowledge-sharing. By contributing to open-source projects, you not only enhance your skills but also contribute to the collective progress of the community. It's a dynamic space where innovation thrives through shared efforts, creating a more inclusive and robust digital landscape.

Sustainable Future

Promoting renewable energy adoption, reducing single-use plastics, and implementing circular economy practices are pivotal for a greener world. Sustainable urban planning, afforestation initiatives, and widespread environmental education further contribute to a healthier planet. Embracing these solutions collectively fosters a harmonious balance between human activities and the Earth's well-being.

Smart Automation

Automation will redefine industries through AI-driven processes, from autonomous vehicles in transportation to smart manufacturing in production lines. In healthcare, robotic surgery and AI diagnostics will enhance precision and efficiency. Across domains, the future of automation promises increased productivity, cost-effectiveness, and innovation.

Rules and Guidelines

  • This is a team participation hackathon. Hackers must form a team of 2 - 4 members.
  • The hackathon will start on November 27, 2023, at 10:00 AM IST, and end on November 28th, 2023, at 10:00 AM IST, you work on your hack during the allotted time only. Older projects will not be allowed. We will require you to build your project on the hackathon weekend.
  • It's an in-person hackathon, you can participate from anywhere but have to come to the venue.
  • There are 4 themes of the hackathon, you must submit a hack that is in one of these themes.
  • It is mandatory for every member of a team to be registered for the hackathon on Devpost and Join Discord.
  • Hackers are not allowed to work on multiple teams and/or submit multiple projects. Hackers must be a part of only one team and submit only one project.
  • Once the hackathon starts, you will get an option to submit your hack, you can submit as many times as you want, and the last hack will be considered as the final submission.
